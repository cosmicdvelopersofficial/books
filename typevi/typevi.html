Type VI civilization and Time Travelling

A type VI civilization in the Kardashev scale, is believed to achieve time travel. However, how will that be possible? Let’s analyze. A type VI civilization can control multiple universes. Humanity is not expected to reach type VI ever. Some problems of time travelling are undoing an action in the past because everything can change, paradoxes (eg. “Grandfather Paradox”) and fast forwarding to the future, attain information, and let people in the present know. A few reasons why our civilization cannot time travel: Humanity has not found a solution to any of the above, and that each decision one makes can branch into a different future. Here, the quantum suicide theory comes in. Each time someone chooses something, the universe splits in half. So, how does the Type VI time travel? As it’s mentioned previously, a type VI can control multiple universes. So, a good idea would be to travel to the other half of the universe with the changed decision. For example: When the Milky-Way and Andromeda galaxy collision occurs, I will have two universes. The one where the galaxies have collided and the other one in which they haven’t.
