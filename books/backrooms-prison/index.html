Could the Backrooms level “Run for Your Life” be used for a sentence?

The Backrooms have many levels. One of the hardest levels is the level “Run for Your Life”. What is this level all about? Level ! (Run for your life) has the following info:

Survival Difficulty: Class V

Unsafe

Unsecure

Entity Infestation

Level ! consists of a long hallway, approximately 10 km in length, having the lookalike of a hospital. Flashing red lights are present at even intervals along the hallway, along with an audible emergency alarm noise. At random intervals, doors are present along the hallway, but all have been locked thus far.

                Level ! has many exits:

·         Running 10km to the end of the level will transport you back to the level you entered or back at Level 0.

·         Instead, going towards the entities would lead you to the Void, a place where you do not want to be.

·         Dying in this level has a 0.4% chance of leading into Level Rush

·         Noclipping through the floor will lead to level 52

·         Noclipping through the hallway may lead to The End

·         Staying has a small chance of leading to Level X. Do not attempt.

Technically, escaping from the whole backrooms is almost impossible, so the Backrooms could be used as prisons in the future so that imprisoned people cannot escape.

Level ! can be accessed directly by only one way. Going through faulty elevators. Meaning, that imprisoning someone into the Level ! can be almost impossible.

It is believed that this will be achieved in the near future, where we will have the capability to send someone to Level ! on purpose, and where more serious crimes will be done, meaning higher punishments. Unfortunately, if the prisoner ends up being innocent, there will be no way out.
